I was aware but unaware

That, what brain reeks,

can transpire in grimace;

And what heart seeks,

can only be found in solace;

I throw questions

that I can't unask;

I regurgitate answers

I can never take back;

In this goading duet,

lies a hidden melancholy;

And when the storm subsides,

You'll realize that it was all a folly;
