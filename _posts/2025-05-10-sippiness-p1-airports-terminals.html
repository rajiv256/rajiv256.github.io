Waiting in airports late into the night or the early dawn is an experience that is between normal and paranormal. It is the same kind one would experience during a skydive. Or perhaps while sipping hot tea on a porch when it is pouring outside. You are far from the hustle and bustle of the world, with occasional passengers rushing across. But mostly there is nothing pulling you down from imagining a world that lies outside those glass walls. A life that awaits you. It instills a sense of bravado.

siPPi felt a similar sense of bravado as he sat with his leg up in a secluded corner inside the airport. He is on a seven-hour layover for his next flight that takes him to the United States.
