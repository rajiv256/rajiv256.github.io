I skip the seconds

Waiting for the chance

To steal a glance;

Smirking at the infirmity

To hold my ground

When you’re around;

With eyes for a kaleidoscope

I see gilded prisms

In every day’s monotony;

While the chirps of the birds

appeared to be in a tune

I could finally see

The bright side of the moon;

Persisting the revere in my sight

Resisting a river in my walk

I lied on the rooftop

Counting stars

Dazed and confused!
