(During the vacation... In college , near CCD.)

Boy : Hi! How are you? What are you doing in the college during vacation ?

Girl : Hi ! Is this a coincidence ?

Boy : You made me believe in that concept. !

Girl : Oh! Sure! I am good. I am here on a project. By the way which department are you in?

Boy : As if you don't know.

Girl : Ok then, I am leaving. B..

Boy : Do you wanna have coffee? Given that we are very much near to its source.

Girl : No, I don't drink coffee.

Boy : Ok. Lets put it this way. When you go out on your first date, what would you like to have?

Girl : I have no plans whatsoever, but Iced tea would be good.

Boy : But we can't get Iced tea here!!

Girl : Likewise. When you guys have creative ways of asking a girl out, shouldn't we have creative ways to reject? ;)

Girl : Ok! We will see.

Boy : So, can I walk with you till your hostel?

Girl : Of course. You can walk on the road whichever side you want.

Boy : You know what, Too much sarcasm from a girl can drive a guy crazy. I wanna walk on the other side. See you. Bye.
