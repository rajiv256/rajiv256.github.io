**Note: Raw draft of a screenplay written in Telugu.** (long time no see :3)

Raining heavily. A decently rich looking apartment in Hyderabad. Apartment ground floor lo inka cement bastaalu alaane padunnai. Inka sunnaalu vesthu unnaaru apartments lo. Stairs meeda kooda akkadakkada varsham tumparlu padtunnai. Tucked pinkish hues shirt and black pant and black belt, hadavidi ga sardina hair, konchem baane maintain chestunna beard tho Uday kindaki hadaavidi ga digutunnaadu. Pakkane unna shop lo evo vastuvulu konukkovaali ani. Daarilo oka neighbour ammai (pakkinti parimala) tho chalaaki ga maatlaaduthuu kindaki digutunnaadu. Aame kooda tanaki konchem mundu stairs lo digutundi, tana daggara umbrella unna share cheyyaalante bhayamesi dooram nunche maatlaadutu veltundi.

Finally, Uday kindaki vacchesaadu. Appudu realise ayyaadu, tanu anukunnadaanikante varsham konchem gattigaa ne padtundi ani. Konchem tadabadi, aa tarvaata edaite adaindi le ani saahasam chesi pakkana unna house ki extended roof untey, first aithe daani kindaki veldaam, ala ala inka support teeskuni velpodaam anukunnaadu. Mana brathukki godugu teeskuney opika untey inkemi anukuntunnaadu manasulo.

Meanwhile, pakkinti parimala, tana mundu nunchey godugu tippukuntuu veltundi. Naaku time vastundi, appudu cheptha annatlu choosaadu tana vaipu. Inthalo, tana mundu nunchi, “hey, malli lift kaavaala?” Ani adigindi, intha baaru godugu, antakante baaraina navvutho, Subha.

**Subha: ** Subha instagram lo travel influencer. Kaani tanu migata influencers la rich places ki vellakunda, andhra lo ni villages ki velli akkade konnaallu stay chesi akkadi culture ni, choodatagga pradesaalni cover chestundi. Inka tana maatalu chaala chalaaki ga untai. Following kooda gattigaane untundi. Subha’s skin tone nalupu. Konchem nalupu chaamanchaaya kalipinattlu untundi. Tana parents tega benga padipoyevaallu eppudu. Tanu History and Anthropology chadivindi College lo. Maybe, vividha vidhaalaina prajala gurinchi study cheyyadam vallano, lokam gurinchi oka broad outlook undadam vallano, chaala pustakaalu chadavadam vallano kaani, chinnappudu friends nalla ga unnavani edipinchina, peddayyaaka tanatho tanu comfortable ayipoyindi.

Uday tana friend Rishi pelli kosam hyderabad vacchaadu. Ee apartment lo Rishi bandhuvulu untunnaru. Vaalla ammaye ee pakkinti parimala. Uday, Rishi chinnappati nunchi best friends. Kaani Rishi arts, Uday sciences. Ala kontha dooram perigindi kaani eppuduu touch lo ne undevaallu. Subha Rishi same college. Same apartment lo untaaru kooda, ala manchi friends aipoyyaaru. Appudappudu Rishi, Subha daggara Uday gurinchi maatlaadevaadu. Ala Subha ki Uday evaro telsu. Ila pelliki vacchinappudu first time kalisindi.

Pelli time lo Rishi side relatives lo oka chinna paapa kanapadatledu. Uday Subha paanipuri fountain daggara maatlaadukuntu untey, Rishi meghana (aa chinna paapa) kanapadatledu anta, choodandi ani photo choopinchaadu. Phone lo photo teeskunnadu Uday. Uday, Subha oka team la pani chesi, finally aa paapa store room lo by mistake velpoyindi and daaniki taalaalu vesesaaru ani kanipedtaaru. Make a good team. Marriage malli normalcy ki restored. Marriage aipoyyaka Rishi vacchi, thanks chepthuu, inkonnaallu aagi vellu ani cheppi tana flats lo ne inko apartment key istaadu. Konchem atu itu tippu veedni ani Subha toh cheptaadu. Subha, em cheyyaalanukuntunnaav ante, charminar chowrasta lo irani chai annaadu Uday. Na car lo veldaam ani car teestuntey, Neekosam ippudu akkada 6 lane road lu estaara, ani tana scooty bayataki teesindi Subha.

Cut to present

Malli lift kaavaala ani adigina Subha navvu, tana pedaalu choosi Uday, “haa ivvu, US varaku ilaane veldaam”, annaadu. Aa maata tho Subha navvu mottha face nundi vellipoyindi. Malli konchem navvuthu, “ivvalena nee flight?” Ani adigindi. Uday tana tone ni konchem pasigatti, “avunu”, ani next extended roof daggaraki direct chesaadu. Tanani choostu, “vellodda nenu?” Ani adigaadu. Konchem encouraged ga Subha, “vellu le, but inkonni roojulu untey baagundu anipistundi, enduko mari”, andi. “Naakkooda nuvvante konchem ishtam create ayyindi”, annaadu Uday, tana kallaloki choostu. “Avna, em observe chesaav first?” Ani Subha chilipi ga adigindi. Ee time lo vaalliddari shoulders contact lo unnai, godugu peddadaina, daggaragaane nilchunnaaru. “First aite nee pedaalu, tarvaata kallu”, annaadu Uday. Varsham prabhaavamo, oka week days nunchi kalisi tirugutunna, eppudu intimate time ni evaro okaru spoil cheyyadam vallano, ivvaala vidipothaam ane bhayam vallano, iddaru okarni okaru chooskuntunnaaru, Subha Uday nadumu ni patkundi, Uday left hand tho godugu ni kindaki dinchi, right hand tana nadumu paina vesaadu. Mellaga kiss cheskunnaaru. Their chests were facing each other. Ala konchem sepati tarvaata, separate ga Uday ki icchina room ki vellaaru. Inkonchem sepu kiss cheskunnaaru. Bathroom ki vellaaru. Bodies wash cheskunnaaru. Then, they made love, first snaanam chesaaka, and bed room lo continue chesaaru. Aipoyyaaka malli velli bodies wash cheskunnaaru. They each helped clean other and malli head bath chesaaru. Uday hair ki tanu shampoo chestundi, and Subha body ni Uday carress chestu soap apply chestunnaadu.

Baytaki vacchaaka, aakali vestundi, edaina cook cheskundaam annaadu. Kaani illu tanadi kaadu kaabatti, iddaru car lo velli chicken curry stuff tecchukuni, cheskuni tinnaaru. Both took a nap, Subha tana tala Uday chest meeda, kaalu Uday kaalu meeda pettindi. Uday, Subha kaalu meeda kaalu vesaadu. Ala evening varaku padkunnaaru. Evening Uday flight ki time ayyindi. “Ippudenti next”, ani Uday Subha ni adigaadu. Subha, “nuvvippudu vellipothunte nenem cheptaanu”, ani andi. “Naakaite neetho undaalani undi, konni roojulu long distance aina parledu”, annaadu. Subha, “nenippudu em cheppalenu. Anukodaaniki baane untadi kaani long distance, naa mind set ki set avvademo anipistundi andi”. “Sare, nenu anukundi nenu cheppaanu, no pressure.”, ani tana vastuvulu sardukovadam modalupettaadu Uday. “Nuvvu velpo Subha, nenu Uber lo veltaanu”, annaadu Uday, dress up avthu, bathroom lo fresh avtunna Subha tho. “Ledu ledu, Rishi car undi kadaa, nenu drop chestaanu”, andi subha towel tho moham tuduchukuntu, “and ivvaalla em jarigindi ani evartho anaku, especially Rishi tho. Cheppavu, but just cheptunna”, andi. Uday, thumbsup icchaadu. Car drive silent ga jarigindi. Airport drop off daggariki velthu Uday pakka seat lo unna Subha cheyyi patkuni, “I wish we had some more days”, annaadu, “Kallu tadi ga ayyaayi”. Subha, “naakkooda”, andi. Luggage dinchukunnaaka, Subha Uday ni gattiga hug cheskuni cheek meeda two times gattiga kiss chesindi. Uday reciprocated. “Ok bye”, ani cheppi Uday terminal lopaliki vellipoyaadu. Subha car lo venakki vellipoyindi. 10 mins tarvaata check-in line lo unna Uday ki Subha nunchi oka message vacchindi:

I’ll miss you

~ Subha
