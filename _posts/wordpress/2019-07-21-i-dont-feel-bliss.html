What is life
But a perpetual struggle of
Waking to sleep,
And Sleeping, yet to be awake in dreams.

Existence is a lie,
We just linger
As a puppet of our emotions;
Engorged into the life's fabric is this lie
That everyone are too afraid to deny.

A dirty veil, you so conspicuously hide behind

Stuck in the dogma of your butchered self

Whatever the hell that self is

There is pain

But you don't want to feel it

There is suffering

But you choose to blindside it

Instead,

You play with butterflies behind that veil

And call that liberation

You crucified Christ,
Gave poison to Socrates,
Oppressed unruly thoughts
And sold your soul for misinterpreted glory
But that's ok, ain't it?
Coz now, You are drowned in the sea of lies
flowing with the current
Inevitably stuck in a loop
Neither a thought of getting out
Nor a desire to

Emotions are to blame
They are responsible
They act as your friend
They show false promises
A part of you still know that
In the end, all hearts are broken
But they show you false faith,
Instant gratification, occasional victories
To ensure you, that everything is going good
And in this process,
They suck the life blood out of you

What is life,
But a mosaic of memories;
Untagged by emotions, You will find Order ab Chaos
A learned friend of mine once said
Consciousness is a tragic misstep in human evolution
We became too self aware
The most honourable thing for us to do as a species
Is to walk hand-in-hand into extinction
And honey, that's the reason why
I don't feel Bliss.

~ Inspired by the lonely dark night spent at the foot of a hill beside a lake.
