I'd like to thrift

(For wacky revels in some tacky jewels)

She does it monthly;

I'd like to organise

(For pensive rooms with fancy perfumes)

She does it weekly;

I'd like to read

(For gripping prose ou dripping wit)

She does it everyday;

I'd like to love her

(For stolen glances and slow dances)

She does it all the time.
