The growth of data centers is inevitable. A huge environmental concerns
regarding data centers are (a) their effect on the neighboring electric
grid and (b) their water consumption. I read on the news that AI firms
are investing into nuclear energy. There is a thought on putting data
centers in space so that they can leverage unbridled solar energy. But
the problem of water consumption is still there. The “beginnings” of my
potential solution is to build a sort of a “greenhouse” that traps the
evaropated water and condense it back for reuse. Essentially, perhaps,
the data center is constructed inside an artificially constructed water
body and then the whole area is surrounded in a greenhouse so that the
water used for cooling evaporates and then condenses back.
